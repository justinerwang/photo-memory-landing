Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Information We Collect

If you join the Photo Memory waitlist, we collect the email address you provide and the optional archive size description you enter.

How We Use Information

We use waitlist information to send product updates, early access invitations, launch information, and related support messages.

Service Providers

Waitlist submissions are processed by Formspree, which receives and stores the form data submitted through this website.

Local App Data

The Photo Memory app is designed to scan selected local folders on your Mac. It stores its search index, cached thumbnails, scan state, and audit logs locally on your device. Original photos stay in their existing folders and are not uploaded by default.

Local audit logs can include app actions such as adding sources, scanning, exporting selected files by copy, duplicate review actions, Finder handoffs, and optional error-reporting consent changes. You can export the local activity log from the app for support or review.

Duplicate Review Handoff

Duplicate review happens inside the app. The app can show duplicate groups with thumbnails and links that open Finder at the original file location so you can inspect files yourself. Photo Memory does not delete, move, rename, trash, recover, or clean up original files.

Optional Error Reporting

Optional error reporting is off by default. If it is enabled, the app shows a local preview of the report before submission. Error reports are intended for debugging and can include app version, local archive health counts, redacted error messages, source-code file and line references, and sanitized backtraces.

Error reports are designed to exclude photos, thumbnails, full indexes, face embeddings, license keys, full file paths, and search text. Error report opt-in, opt-out, preview, and submit attempts are recorded in the local audit log.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only with service providers needed to operate the waitlist or when required by law.

Your Choices

You may request deletion of your waitlist information by emailing support@v2rstudio.com.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@v2rstudio.com.
